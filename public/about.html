Why Color Palette

You're probably wondering why our team dedicated a page to extracting colors. From a design-thinking perspective, our team values consistency, and with leveraging a color palette, we believe that this can best assist our clientele in creating amazing visual products. However, unlike our competitors, you won't have to download an app, this service is available to you at the click of a button. This application utilizes your inputs into our secure database and leverages the Colormind API to extract any significant color schemes from the image that you upload.
